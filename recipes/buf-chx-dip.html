Buffalo Chicken Dip

Buffalo Chicken Dip is a Super Bowl classic and absolute must have. It is a creamy dip composed of yummy chicken and spicy buffalo sauce. It is a great snack and the perfect game day snack.

  • 1 Chicken Breast
  • 1 8oz Creamcheese Block
  • 1/2 cup of shredded colby jack cheese
  • 1/2 cup of ranch
  • 1/2 cup of Franks Buffalo Sauce
