Buffalo Chicken Dip

Buffalo Chicken Dip is a Super Bowl classic and absolute must have. It is a creamy dip composed of yummy chicken and spicy buffalo sauce. It is a great snack and the perfect game day snack.

Ingredients

  • 1 Chicken Breast
  • 1 8oz Creamcheese Block
  • 1/2 cup of shredded colby jack cheese
  • 1/2 cup of ranch
  • 1/2 cup of Franks Buffalo Sauce

Steps

  1. Boil chicken until fully cooked. Shred and add to mixing bowl.
  2. Preheat oven to 350.
  3. Add cream cheese, ranch, buffalo sauce, and cheese to the bowl. Mix completly.
  4. Add mixture to grease 9x11 baking dish and cook for 20 minutes stirring every 10 mins.
