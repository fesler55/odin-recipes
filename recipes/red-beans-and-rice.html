Read Beans and Rice

Red beans and rice is a cheap and easy meal great for any family.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of Kidney Beans
  • 1/2 package of Johnson Bratwurst
  • 1/2 can of rotel
  • 1 cup uncooked rice
  • 2 cups water
  • salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Add rice and water to rice cooker and start it.
  2. While rice is cooking, pull casing off brats and cook ground up.
  3. Once rice and brats are cooked, combine kidney beans, brats, rice, and rotel into 1 bowl. Mix thoughly
  4. salt and pepper to taste
